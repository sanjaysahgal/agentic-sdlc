# agentic-sdlc — Executive Overview

## What it is

agentic-sdlc is an AI-powered system that takes a feature from idea to shipped code through a structured, gated sequence. Every step is assisted by an AI specialist. Every step requires human approval before the next begins.

## The business problem

Software teams consistently struggle with the same failures: features that weren't fully understood before building started, design that happened after engineering instead of before it, specs that existed but weren't followed, and handoffs where critical context was lost. These failures are expensive — wasted engineering time, rework, and features that don't match what users actually needed.

agentic-sdlc makes the pre-build sequence — from idea to ready-to-build — rigorous and fast. Teams spend less time building the wrong thing.

## How it works

A product manager starts by describing a feature idea in a Slack channel. An AI specialist asks questions, pushes back, and helps shape a clear, complete product spec. When the PM is satisfied, they approve it. The spec is saved. A UX designer is automatically notified that design work can begin. The designer works with an AI design specialist to produce screens and flows. When the designer approves, engineering can begin — with a clear spec and complete designs already done.

No phase starts until the previous one is approved. No code is written without a complete engineering plan. No engineering plan is written without complete designs. No design starts without a complete product spec.

## What teams get

**Speed where it matters.** The AI specialists do the heavy lifting — asking the right questions, drafting and redrafting, surfacing edge cases. Humans make decisions and approve. The spec phase that used to take weeks of meetings takes hours of focused conversation.

**No more "what were we thinking."** Every decision is captured in the spec. Every spec is version-controlled. You can always see exactly what was decided, when, and why.

**Scale without chaos.** The same workflow that works for one person works for 500 engineers. Feature teams work in parallel channels with no coordination overhead. The system enforces the sequence.

**Roles are protected.** Designers design. Engineers engineer. The system prevents engineering from starting before design is done. It prevents design from starting before the product spec is approved. Each role does their part at the right time.

## What it is not

It is not a project management tool. It does not replace GitHub Issues, Linear, or Jira — it integrates with them.

It is not an autonomous coding system. Humans make every decision. AI specialists assist and accelerate.

It does not require replacing your existing Slack workspace or GitHub setup. It connects to what you already have.

## Standalone product

agentic-sdlc is a separate product from any app it helps build. A team brings their GitHub repository, their Slack workspace, and their Anthropic API key. agentic-sdlc provides the full workflow — from the AI specialists to the spec formats to the Slack interface. It works for health apps, fintech apps, developer tools, or any software product.
